# Commonplace

**Applied integration for senior leaders designing the next generation of organisations.**

Commonplace Ventures is a working studio in the tradition of the **commonplace book** — a scholar's notebook for collecting and integrating knowledge across many domains. What that tradition was to a Renaissance polymath, this practice is to a modern senior leader: an accumulated stock of observation, framework, and judgment, brought fresh to the problem at hand.

Two decades of observation across tech leadership, enterprise advisory, product and platform work, and organisation design — now brought into service of leaders rethinking the shape of their companies in the age of intelligence.

---

## What this is

Most consulting today is applied *frameworks*. Commonplace is applied *integration*.

- Not an AI strategy practice. An **organisation design practice** for an era when AI makes organisations possible in new shapes.
- Not a transformation programme vendor. A **diagnostic partner** — COM-B-grade rigour on where change is stuck and why.
- Not a deck shop. A **thinking practice** — long-form essays, field-tested frameworks, rigorous diagnosis.

## Who it's for

Senior operators — COO, CEO, CTPO, CTO, Head of Product Engineering — at companies that have moved past "should we use AI" and are now sitting with the harder question: *what does our organisation need to become so intelligence (human + machine) flows?*

## How it engages

1. **Diagnosis** — named problem, unique mechanism, specific evidence. 1-2 weeks, fixed fee.
2. **Retainer** — ongoing senior thinking partner. £10-25K/month.
3. **Intensives** — focused sessions with senior teams. Project-scoped.
4. **Writing** — essays, field notes, framework releases. Public.
