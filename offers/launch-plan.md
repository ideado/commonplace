## Phase 2 — Test positioning (W17, 20-26 Apr)

**Target: 5 positioning conversations. No pitching. Pure diagnosis + feedback.**

### Format

- 30-40 min calls
- Script: *"I'm narrowing what I do next. Can you stress-test how I'm describing it?"*
- Present offer 1-pager. Ask: *What resonates? What confuses? What would make you refer me?*
- Close with: *"If you heard of someone sitting with X problem, who comes to mind?"*

### Contacts to target

Pull from:
- 1,515 LinkedIn connections — filter senior (Director+), in target industries
- EE network — senior operators who've moved to in-house
- Previous client-side contacts
- Friends-of-friends with CXO roles

Prioritise: people who **would hire this work themselves** if they had a real problem *right now*.

### Output by end of W17

- Refined offer 1-pager v1.0
- 3 names of "would introduce" prospects
- 1-2 follow-up conversations that have "let's talk about this for me" energy

## Phase 3 — First engagement (W18-W19, 27 Apr – 10 May)

**Target: First paid diagnosis signed.**

### Offer architecture (Diagnosis)

- **What:** 2-week organisational diagnosis. Named problem, COM-B-grade analysis, written report, 90-min exec readout.
- **Fixed fee:** £7,500 – £12,000 (start at £7,500, move to £10k+ as proof accumulates).
- **Why fixed fee:** lowers the buyer's decision threshold, forces your clarity, creates a natural "next phase" conversation.
- **Why diagnosis:** pure diagnosis is the cleanest first sale. You're not competing with transformation programmes. You're doing something specific nobody else is selling.

### Target buyer signal

Any of these is enough:
- Stalled AI / transformation initiative
- Just appointed to a role where they inherit an org that doesn't work
- Pre-IPO pressure to show operating leverage
- Recent all-hands where people asked "how does AI change what we do" — and they had no answer

### Close mechanics

- Send the offer 1-pager as a link (hosted on the site, not a PDF attachment — shows quality)
- Follow with 30-min scoping call
- Proposal in plain doc form within 24 hours, with fixed fee and timeline
- Invoice on signature (50% upfront, 50% on readout)

## Phase 4 — Retainer conversion (W20-W22, 11-31 May)

**Target: First £10K/mo+ retainer signed.**

A successful diagnosis naturally ends with *"so what should I do about this?"* That's the retainer door.

### Retainer offer shape

- **Name:** *Advisory Retainer*
- **What:** 2 half-days / month senior thinking partnership + async availability
- **Fee:** £10-15K/mo, 3-month minimum, then month-to-month
- **Structure:** 2 × 90-min sessions, plus ~1h/wk async Slack/email response, plus 1 written artefact per month (memo, one-pager, thinking document)

### Conversion script

During the diagnosis readout:
> *"You'll get the full report with recommendations. Implementing them is its own work. Some clients run that themselves. Some want me in the room as it happens — once or twice a month for 90 minutes, between-session availability, and one written thinking-piece each month tailored to what's live. If that's useful, we can set it up starting next month."*
