## The problem

Most organisations have moved past "should we use AI."

They're now sitting with the harder question: *why isn't it working the way we expected?*

AI tools are live. Features are shipping. And yet — the coordination tax keeps rising, decisions still queue at the top, and the org that was supposed to absorb new capabilities looks exactly like it did three years ago.

The issue isn't the technology. It's the organisation design.

---

## What's happening

When you bolt AI onto an org that was shaped for a different era, you get:

- Intelligence that can't flow — because the structure blocks it
- Leaders overloaded with decisions that should be made closer to the work
- Transformation programmes that stall because the *capability* is there but the *context* isn't
- Teams shipping AI features that their own organisation can't operationalise

This isn't a tools problem. It's a shape problem.

---

## What Commonplace Ventures does

A rigorous, named diagnosis of where your organisation is misshapen for the intelligence era — and a clear picture of what it needs to become.

Not a framework deck. Not a workshop series. A working diagnostic: your specific org, your specific problem, your specific next move.

**Who it's for:** One senior leader (CEO, COO, CTPO, CTO) with a live transformation problem and the authority to act on the findings.

**What you get:**
- A named, specific diagnosis of where change is stuck and why
- The behavioural and structural mechanisms driving the stall
- A clear picture of the organisation that wants to emerge
- A 90-minute readout with your senior team
- The written report as a working artefact — not a one-time presentation

**Fixed fee:** £7,500 – £10,000
