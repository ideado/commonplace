## Product Vision

> *The applied integration layer for senior leaders redesigning organisations in the intelligence era — combining accumulated judgment (Think) with hands-on delivery capability (Build).*

Commonplace Ventures is both a **thinking practice** and a **delivery studio**. The product isn't software — it's packaged expertise: diagnostic engagements, retainer relationships, written frameworks, and built capabilities. The CTPO role here governs the product architecture of the practice itself: what we offer, how we deliver it, how we improve it, how we tell the story.

### Offer evolution (Q3-Q4)

- **Diagnosis v2** — add COM-B worksheet as a client-facing artefact. Makes the methodology visible.
